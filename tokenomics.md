# TERC20 Tokenomics

**TERC20** is an ERC20 token on the **BSC (Binance Smart Chain)**, built to support community participation, staking, and NFT initiatives.  

**Total Supply:** 100,000,000,000 TERC20  

**Note:** TERC20 does not provide for a public sale — tokens are distributed through giveaways, staking, and NFT initiatives to support the community and ecosystem.

---

## Distribution

| Category | Percent | Quantity (TERC20) | Description |

| Community and community sections | 50% | 50,000,000,000 | Airdrops, X activities, Telegram, Discord, bonuses for the Cat Lovers Club. |
| Staking and Rewards | 20% | 20,000,000,000 | Staking programs and bonuses for long‑term holders (from November 2025). |
| NFT ecosystem | 15% | 15,000,000,000 | Reserve for the TERC20 Meme Vault collection and other NFT initiatives. |

| Project reserve | 5% | 5,000,000,000 | For future developments and unforeseen expenses. |

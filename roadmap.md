# TERC20 Roadmap

## Phase 1 – The Foundation Begins
**July 2025 – September 2025**

- Token price starts at $1 and launches on the **BSC blockchain** for fast transactions  
- Launch of the cross-chain bridge for stablecoins  
- Official launch of the **TERC20 token**  
- Giveaways to engage the community  
- Wallet integration: MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Phantom, and others  
- Smart contract audit: Verification by independent auditors (e.g., CertiK)  
- Launch marketing: Meme campaigns and viral content on X and TikTok  

---

## Phase 2 – Building Momentum
**September 2025 – October 2025**

- Community vote: Holders with 10,000+ TERC20 decide on token burning (up to 30%) and easing the dollar peg  
- Launch of **TERC20 Meme Vault**: Exclusive NFTs with memes, art, and hidden utility  
- Holders Club: Exclusive access for top holders (bonuses, NFTs, closed events)  
- Partnerships: Collaborations with BSC projects (DeFi, NFT, metaverse)  
- Active marketing: AMAs, meme contests, NFT giveaways for community growth  

---

## Phase 3 – Breaking Free
**November 2025 onwards**

- Large-scale token burning: Up to 30% of tokens (~20–30 billion TERC20)  
- Complete decoupling from the dollar: Free market rate, preparing for a price rally  
- Launch of NFT marketplace: **TERC20 Meme Vault** NFT trading, auction, and staking platform  
- Staking program: Rewards for holders (20% of tokens, 20 billion TERC20) with bonuses for long-term participants  
- Ecosystem expansion: Integration with DeFi protocols, games, and metaverses on BSC  
- Global marketing: Strengthening presence on X, TikTok, and partnerships with influencers

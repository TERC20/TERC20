\section*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Abstract}
TERC20 is a decentralized stablecoin project built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) aiming to provide fast transactions, community engagement, and innovative NFT initiatives. The project follows a phased roadmap to empower the community and expand the ecosystem while offering staking, rewards, and a unique Meme Vault NFT experience.

% --- Roadmap ---
\section{TERC20 Roadmap}

\begin{roadmapbox}{Phase 1 – The Foundation Begins (July 2025 – Sept 2025)}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Token price starts at \$1; launch on BSC for fast transactions.
    \item Launch of the cross-chain bridge for stablecoins.
    \item Official launch of the TERC20 token.
    \item Giveaways to engage the community.
    \item Wallet integration: MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Phantom, etc.
    \item Smart contract audit: Verification by independent auditors (e.g., CertiK).
    \item Launch marketing: Meme campaigns and viral content on X and TikTok.
\end{itemize}
\end{roadmapbox}
\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{roadmapbox}{Phase 2 – Building Momentum (Sept 2025 – Oct 2025)}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Community vote: Holders with 10,000+ TERC20 decide on token burning (up to 30\%) and easing the dollar peg.
    \item Launch of TERC20 Meme Vault: Exclusive NFTs with memes, art, and hidden utility.
    \item Holders Club: Exclusive access for top holders (bonuses, NFTs, closed events).
    \item Partnerships: Collaborations with BSC projects (DeFi, NFT, metaverse).
    \item Active marketing: AMAs, meme contests, NFT giveaways for community growth.
\end{itemize}
\end{roadmapbox}
\vspace{0.5cm}

\begin{roadmapbox}{Phase 3 – Breaking Free (Nov 2025 onwards)}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Large-scale token burning: Up to 30\% of tokens ($\sim$20–30 billion TERC20).
    \item Complete decoupling from the dollar: Free market rate, preparing for a price rally.
    \item Launch of NFT marketplace: TERC20 Meme Vault NFT trading, auction, and staking.
    \item Staking program: Rewards for holders (20\% of tokens) with bonuses for long-term participants.
    \item Ecosystem expansion: Integration with DeFi protocols, games, and metaverses.
    \item Global marketing: Strengthening presence on X, TikTok, and influencer partnerships.
\end{itemize}
\end{roadmapbox}
